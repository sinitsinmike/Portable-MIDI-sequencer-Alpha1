## What It Is

A budget-friendly standalone MIDI sequencer designed for **live MIDI performance recording**, multi-track sequencing, and reliable project-based workflow — without requiring a computer.

Designed as a **centerpiece of a dawless setup** in its Alpha stage.

---

## Key Capabilities

- Live MIDI recording (linear + loop modes)
- 16 tracks / 16 MIDI channels
- Multi-device routing via MIDI router
- Project save/load/clean (flash-based, robust)
- Minimal screen gazing workflow
- Encoder + 8 buttons + 8 LEDs
- 0.96" OLED display
- MIDI TRS In / Out (DIN via adapter)

---

## Alpha 1 Focus

- Practical evaluation of the core sequencing workflow
- Collecting focused user feedback to guide future architectural and workflow decisions

---

## Roadmap Highlights (Planned)

- MIDI Clock In / Out
- Track mute / solo
- Scrub mode
- Full MIDI CC control of device functions
- Larger display variant (2.4" SSD1309)

## Target Audience

Musicians comfortable with live playing on MIDI keyboards or pads, seeking a **hands-on hardware sequencer** rather than grid-based programming.
