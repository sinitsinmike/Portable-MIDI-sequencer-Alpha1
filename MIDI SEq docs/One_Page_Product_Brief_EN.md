# Portable MIDI Sequencer — Alpha 1
## One-page Product Brief

### What It Is
A budget-friendly, standalone hardware MIDI sequencer designed for live MIDI recording
and dawless music creation.

### Core Capabilities
- Live MIDI performance recording
- 16 tracks / 16 MIDI channels
- Multi-instrument control via MIDI routing
- Reliable save/load system
- Internal clock (MIDI Clock In/Out planned)

### Design Intent
Designed as a centerpiece for dawless setups during Alpha 1,
with future updates expanding synchronization and control capabilities.

### Target Users
- Hardware-focused musicians
- Dawless performers
- Keyboard and pad players

### Price Positioning
- Target range: $100–150 USD
- Lower cost at higher production volumes
- Higher cost for limited or manual assembly runs

### Future Expansion
- MIDI Clock synchronization
- MIDI CC control of core features
- Advanced track controls
- Enhanced display options
